Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers for planning your visit to ILHAN RESTAURANT.

How do I reserve a table?

Use the reservation page to select your date, time, and number of guests. You can also call us directly for assistance.

Do you offer takeaway?

Yes, selected dishes are available for takeaway. Please contact us in advance for availability.

Do you host private events?

We host private dinners, birthday celebrations, and corporate gatherings. Please contact us for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, card payments, and mobile payment methods for your convenience.

Are reservations required?

Reservations are recommended, especially on weekends and during special occasions.
